# Appendix B — References

Citations for [README.md](README.md), grouped by type.

## Books

- *Designing Data-Intensive Applications*, Martin Kleppmann, O'Reilly 2017 — Ch. 3 (Storage and Retrieval: OLTP vs OLAP, row vs columnar), Ch. 6 (Partitioning: shared-nothing MPP), Ch. 7 (Transactions: ACID), Ch. 9 (Consistency and Consensus: CAP, BASE).
- *Fundamentals of Data Engineering*, Reis & Housley, O'Reilly 2022 — lifecycle framing and engine selection.
